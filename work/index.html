Japantown Incarceration →

[editing + design + development]. An analysis and visualization of how Franklin D. Roosevelt's Executive Order 9066 affected San Francisco's Japantown. I led the design direction and development of this piece. This project received two bronze medals and an award of excellence at the Society for News Design Creative Competition in 2025.
